Membership Tiers

The Non-Profit Membership is for non-profit organizations. No fee is required for this tier.

The Bronze Membership costs $5 a month and includes the Granbury newsletter and coupons to local businesses.

The Silver Membership costs $10 a month. It includes the perks of the Bronze tier and adds on online advertising.

The Gold Membership costs $20 a month. All perks of previous tiers are included. Additionally, gold members receive a dedicated business coach available 24/7 and premier online advertising slots.
