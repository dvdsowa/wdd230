Target Audience

The Basic target audience is the business interested public, be it business owners, employees or customers. Since people who come to the site have different backgrounds, there will be plenty of information that would interest whoever would navigate to the site. There will of course be content to explain what the site is about, what the events the chamber has scheduled and what objectives they are trying to accomplish currently. It is also expected.

Persona 1

Business Bill, Job owner, Age 35-44, Master's Degree in Business, member of facebook, Instagram, Twitter and Pinterest, owner of a Real-Estate firm, 11-50 employees communicates through phone, email, text, social media and meetings. Responsible for managing underlings who sell home and support potential buyers and taking care of business finances. Gets info for Job by going to sales conferences and workshops, receiving calls from potential home sellers and buyers, using social media, billboards, texting and other means to advertise their services and compare the great deals they have gotten for buyers and sellers. Needs the following tools: accounting and bookkeeping systems, content management systems, word processing programs, business intelligence dashboards, crm software, project management, reporting software, employee scheduling software, invoicing software and email. Main goal and measure of success is revenue, and secondarily customer satisfaction. Biggest challenges are professional development, collaboration and creativity and resources.

Persona 2

Johnny Curious; delivery driver; 18-24 years; some college, no degree; member of facebook, linked-in; in the food and beverage industry at a restaurant of 11-50 employees. His preferred methods of communication are phone, text messaging and face-to-face. Job responsibilities are taking orders, preparing and delivering food. He reports to a delivery supervisor. He gains his information for his career from state-certification courses, on-the-job training and college. He uses email, invocing software and an employee scheduling software. His job performance is measured by his efficiency and customer satisfaction. His goal is to start his own business. His biggest challenges are communication, problem solving and decision making, resources and professional development.
