Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Granbury Chamber of Commerce. Granbury is a town in Texas. I selected it because it was the combination of two nearby towns, but it turned out that there was a town in Texas with that name, so I opted for that instead.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to promote the local businesses of Granbury by joining them under a common mission and values, listing them in a directory, allowing future businesses to join and divulging the chamber's activties to the community.

Scenarios

  • 1) How are businesses doing in my area?

  • 2) Are there any local networks or groups that advocate for or help to start up local businesses?

  • 3) How do our local businesses give back to the community?
